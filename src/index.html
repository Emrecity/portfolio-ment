My story, my mission, and why I teach

Personal Info

Hello! I'm Foster Appiah, a certified and passionate educator based in Ashanti Region, Ghana. With 8 years of experience in Junior High School education, I specialize in teaching Computing and English. My mission is to make complex topics accessible, build confidence, and help every student reach their full potential. I am dedicated to fostering a love for learning and technology in my students.

Background & Qualifications

Certification: National Teaching Council(NTC) certified, Ghana Education Service Trained

Education: B.Ed in Junior High School Education, University of Education, Winneba

Experience: 6+ years in public education, 2 years in private education, specializing in Computing and English
